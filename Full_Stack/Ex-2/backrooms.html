About the Game

You've no-clipped through reality and found yourself in the Backrooms—an endless maze of yellowed wallpaper, fluorescent lights, and the constant hum of electricity. Navigate through multiple levels of this liminal space, each more disturbing than the last. Solve puzzles, avoid entities, and find your way back to reality... if you can. The Backrooms are infinite, but your sanity is not.
