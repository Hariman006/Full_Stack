About the Game

Experience the ultimate open-world adventure in Los Santos, a sprawling sun-soaked metropolis full of self-help gurus, starlets, and fading celebrities struggling to stay relevant. Navigate through three distinct protagonists—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—each with their own unique story and abilities. Whether you're pulling off heists, racing through the streets, or exploring the vast wilderness, GTA 5 delivers unparalleled freedom and excitement.
